Welcome to Lucknow

Explore the rich heritage and cultural landmarks of Lucknow, a place steeped in history and tradition.
 
Lucknow is a historical city in northern India, known for its rich heritage, culture, and architectural marvels.It was a significant center
during the Nawabi rule, showcasing a blend of Mughal and Persian influences.The city boasts magnificent architectural gems
like Bara Imambara, Chota Imambara, and Rumi Darwaza, reflecting the grandeur of the bygone era.Intricate designs and
artistic craftsmanship are distinctive features of these monuments.
